🌿Where it Starts🔥

Once upon a time, in a quiet village surrounded by green hills, lived two girls — Zumrad and Qimmat. Zumrad was kind, gentle, and full of warmth. But life had not been easy for her. Her mother had passed away when she was just a child, and her father, though once loving, had remarried a harsh and selfish woman.
