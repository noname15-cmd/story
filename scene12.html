✨The Singing Treasure🎶

Child… what is this?” she whispered, her voice trembling between awe and disbelief. Zumrad looked up, her hands still resting on the crate. “A gift,” she said softly. “Given for kindness, not for asking.” The woman’s gaze moved from her daughter’s face to the heap of gleaming jewels, and something deep within her eyes flickered — wonder, fear, and perhaps the first stirring of hope.
