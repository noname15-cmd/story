🌼The Yellow Door🚪

The old woman laid a gentle hand on Zumrad’s shoulder. “Choose the yellow door,” she whispered softly. Zumrad’s heart fluttered, but she stepped inside without fear. In the quiet room, she discovered a wooden crate, plain yet tied with a ribbon as modest as the cottage itself.
