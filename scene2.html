🌸The Proud Daughter💎

The stepmother brought with her own daughter, Qimmat — proud, lazy, and sharp-tongued. From the very beginning, she disliked Zumrad's gentle nature. While Zumrad worked from sunrise to sunset, Qimmat idled in the garden, dreaming of riches and silk gowns.
